University of Michigan Food Pantry

Welcome to the University of Michigan's Food Pantry Page!

College students and staff are facing alarming rates of food insecurity. The Maize and Blue Cupboard is committed to providing a timely and comprehensive solution for the U-M community. Through resources, educational programs, compassionate support, and more, we empower students to make informed decisions and develop essential life skills.

Services:

  • Produce, dairy, meat, bread, frozen and shelf-stable foods (i.e. rice, cans, tuna, etc.)
  • Dishes, silverware, pots & pans, Tupperware, cutting boards, knives, and other kitchenware.
  • Trash bags, toilet paper, cleaning supplies, hygiene products, school supplies, baby items and more.
